Community Guidelines

Read before creating a petition. Violations may be removed by the platform moderator.

1. No NSFW or sexual content

  • No sexual, pornographic, or explicit content of any kind.
  • No content sexualizing minors under any circumstance — this is never tolerated and may be reported to relevant authorities.

2. No hate speech or harassment

  • No content attacking people based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or nationality.
  • No targeted harassment, doxxing, or threats against individuals.

3. No illegal content

  • No petitions promoting violence, terrorism, or illegal activity.
  • No content facilitating fraud, scams, or illegal goods/services.

4. No spam or misleading content

  • No duplicate petitions posted repeatedly to inflate signature counts.
  • No deliberately false or misleading claims presented as fact.
  • No impersonation of real people, brands, or organizations.

5. Keep it a genuine petition

  • Petitions should advocate for a real cause, change, or request — not serve as generic chat, advertising, or off-topic content.

Moderation

The platform owner may remove petitions that violate these guidelines, with a reason recorded permanently on-chain. Petition creators may remove their own petitions at any time, for any reason.

Petitions live permanently on the Base blockchain once created. Removal hides a petition from this interface but the historical record remains on-chain and publicly viewable via a block explorer — this cannot be changed by anyone, including the platform owner.
